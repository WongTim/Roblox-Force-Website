Hello there! Welcome to our official Roblox Force website! Please have a pleasant look at it through navigation!



Roblox Force is the moniker denoting a band of 4 adventurous members whom are ever-ready to tackle on any genre of games be it Role-playing, simulators, First Person Shooters or even story games! Inspired heavily by the Roblox logo, it consists of a dual of contrasting coloured triangles, as with the letters inside it. Together both figures resemble the silhouette of the original Roblox logo. This logo also represents the leader, bala3imcu and is adopted in his avatar.




Currently this is the standard, default logo of the Force, with black and white colourations. As festivals or events approach, the logo can take on multiple different alternate themes relating to that of the festival.
